How It Works

Ways to explore and organize your emotional geography

Record

Capture emotional moments where they happen through diverse ways.

Visualize

Watch your emotion map emerge. Rearrange and organize records to reveal patterns in your emotional landscape.

Reflect

Discover beauty in small details of life as you explore the chemistry between you and place.

Vision

Imagine discovering yourself through emotional geography

Clusters of Peace

Each morning, record how you feel in that corner café. Week by week, your map fills with moments. Then you see it: clusters of calm forming around your favorite neighborhood. The pattern was always there—waiting to be noticed.

Layers of Meaning

Return to Paris after Turin. Your map whispers: "Why did I feel so different here?" You rediscover that past version of yourself, then return to feel it again. Each visit layers meaning onto place.

City, Refracted

You and a friend arrive in the same city. They wander through cozy neighborhoods while you explore the arts district. Two cities emerge from one. Your map reveals how place is always personal—shaped by where you go, who you meet, what you seek.
